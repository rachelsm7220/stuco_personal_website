LEARNING AND EXPLORING ARE MY PASSION.

Hi, I am Rachel Kim, a junior at Carnegie Mellon University studying product design with a concentration in physical computing.

I study design because I truly believe that design is a powerful tool to solve problems and create meaningful experiences for people. Through designing physical and digital interactions, I hope to deliver well-crafted experiences to people. My motivation to design originates from the transformative power of design that can change the lives of people. I enjoy learning new skills and exploring new views, new people, and new environments from many different interactions.

You can always find me playing with textiles, sketching ideas, doodling, and making products in shop.
